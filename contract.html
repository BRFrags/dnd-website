Player social contract

To have a good time playing D&D the players need to adopt a social contract much like other role-playing games. Below are some of the main rules we have for our players:

  1. Respect your fellow players and DM, their actions, opinions and choices.
  2. D&D is a safe place for a whole range of people (and characters), this is a highly important aspect of the game and club.
  3. Listen to your fellow players and DM, remember it is a team effort.
  4. Say yes as much as possible, it leads to the best times.
  5. The DM is always right (even if they are wrong). It is their world and their rules. If you have comments, speak to your DM respectfully after the session.
